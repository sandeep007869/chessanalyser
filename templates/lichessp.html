LICHESS.ORG PGN DOWNLOAD GUIDE

STEP 1: Go to lichess.org and log in to your account or play without logging in.
,STEP 2: Once you have played a game , go to analyze button shown on the right of the board
STEP 3: Scroll down on this page and click on "Download PGN" button shown on the bottom left.
STEP 4: You can copy and paste this PGN into a text file or directly download the file as it is in your system.
